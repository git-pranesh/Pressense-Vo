## SEO & Content Strategy

### SERP Analysis Completed
Manual competitive research across 9 services identified winning content patterns:
- **AEO-first approach:** 2-3 sentence answers to keyword queries
- **Pricing transparency:** All pages show starting prices
- **Time clarity:** Explicit engagement lengths (30 days, 4 weeks, ongoing)
- **Process over promise:** Step-by-step methodology builds confidence
- **Founder-focused:** Language is founder-to-founder, not corporate jargon
- **FAQ schema:** 5-7 FAQ items on every page matching real founder questions
- **LocalBusiness schema:** All pages include location data for local SEO

### Internal Linking Strategy
- Playbooks link to related service pages (upsell path)
- Service pages link to playbooks (lead magnet path)
- Local pages link back to global service pages (consolidate authority)
- Cross-service linking creates thematic clusters
