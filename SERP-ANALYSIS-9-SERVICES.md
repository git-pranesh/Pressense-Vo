# SERP & AEO Analysis: 9 Service Landing Pages

## Winning Patterns Identified

### 1. Fractional CMO Advisor
**Top Ranker Pattern:**
- **Hero Hook:** "Part-time, executive-level marketing leadership without full-time cost"
- **Key Sections:** Engagement models, experience level (15+ years), cost structure ($1K-$15K), 3-6 month minimum
- **AEO Angle:** Directly explain what fractional CMO is + pricing upfront (removes objection immediately)
- **Unique Angle for Pressense:** Single founder, embedded with founders, outcome-focused (revenue, not just strategy)

**Content Structure (Winning):**
1. Hero: Define fractional CMO + solve founder's first question (cost, time commitment)
2. Problem: "You need strategy but can't afford full-time CMO"
3. Engagement Models: Advisory vs Embedded vs One-off (give options)
4. Who It's For: Growth-stage founders, $1M-8M ARR
5. Pricing Transparency: $2K+ starts, scale based on scope
6. Case Results: Revenue impact, efficiency gains
7. Process: How we embed, what we deliver, timeline
8. FAQ: Difference from agency, ROI expectations, minimum commitment

## Content Principles for All 9 Pages

1. **AEO First**: Every page opens with 2-3 sentence direct answer to the keyword query
2. **Pain → Outcome**: Lead with founder pain (emotion), then show transformation (outcomes)
3. **Pricing Transparency**: Always show starting price (removes objection immediately)
4. **Time Clarity**: Be explicit about engagement length (30 days, 4 weeks, ongoing, etc.)
5. **Who It's NOT For**: Optional but powerful—build trust by saying who shouldn't hire you
6. **Process > Promise**: Show step-by-step how we work (builds confidence)
7. **Founder-Focused**: Language is founder-to-founder, not corporate/jargon
8. **Internal Linking**: Link between related services and playbooks
9. **Local Schema**: All pages include LocalBusiness schema + service area
10. **FAQ Schema**: Every page has 5-7 FAQ items matching real founder questions
